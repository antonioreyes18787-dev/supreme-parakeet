About

Who, why, what

I'm Sam — a product manager who spends a lot of time turning messy work into clean systems, and clean systems into things teams actually use.

This site is the public-facing version of that habit. Every artifact here started as something I needed for my own team — onboarding curriculum, a framework for a planning cycle, a template that was easier to share than re-explain. If it earned a second life, it ends up here.

Use any of it. Steal from it. Fork it. Tell me when something works for you, or when it doesn't. The goal is leverage, not vanity.

— Sam
